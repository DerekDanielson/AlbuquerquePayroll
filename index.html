Local Membership

Benefits of becoming a member of the Albuquerque APA Chapter:

  1. Chapter meetings are an excellent source of information and exchange between payroll professionals.
  2. Advance your career as a payroll professional. ABQAPA offers you the opportunity to join a study group that will prepare you for taking either the Certified Payroll Professional (CPP) or Fundamentals of Payroll Compliance (FPC) exam from the American Payroll Association. These study groups meet weekly and are led by qualified CPPs.
  3. A scholarship is awarded annually to a member desiring to become a CPP/FPC. The scholarship covers the study group cost, books and the exam fee after passing the test.
  4. Contribute to the profession and to the community! The Albuquerque APA encourages its members to get involved.
  5. Reduced rates for luncheons
  6. Members only communications
  7. Certification classes
  8. RCH Credits

Payroll Certifications (CPP/FPC)

Local Certified Payroll Professionals (CPP’s) have volunteered to form a study group to assist and mentor those individuals who are interested in taking the National American Payroll Association’s Certified Payroll Professional examination. These tests are given in April-May and September-October of each year. The group meetings will be held twice a week.


What is the CPP/FPC?

The goals of the APA Certification program are to promote the standard for payroll professionals, to encourage professional growth and individual study by payroll professionals, to provide the standard of requisite knowledge for the payroll professional, to measure by means of the certification examination the attainment and application of that standard, and to recognize formally those colleagues who continue to meet the requirements of the APA Certification Board. The FPC designation is for those without extensive payroll experience, but who wish to further their knowledge. The CPP designation is for those with more payroll experience, who also wish to further their knowledge as well as obtain a recognizable certification in the payroll industry.

Scholarship

The Albuquerque APA Chapter awards a scholarship each year to a person who wants to pursue their CPP or FPC Certification. This scholarship covers the cost of the Study Group enrollment fees, the Payroll Source study book, and the registration fee for testing.
